Sensturo

The future through the senses (Interactive Installation)

Sensturo is an interactive work that aims to create an immersive space in which the spectators explore individually or collectively a possible perception of the future. Through body actions of one or more people, it is possible to live experiences composed of sounds, lights and textures that locate them perceptually in a future space.

  • Date: August 2016
  • Client: La tertulia Museum
  • Category:Interactive Installation
  • Software :Arduino
