Sensturo

The future through the senses (Interactive Installation)

Sensturo is an interactive work that aims to create an immersive space in which the spectators explore individually or collectively a possible perception of the future. Through body actions of one or more people, it is possible to live experiences composed of sounds, lights and textures that locate them perceptually in a future space.

Move On

Interactive rehab system focus in knee injury recovery

Move on is an interactive rehab system focus in knee injury recovery through dynamic routines and virtual tool

Move on is a portable system complementary to rehabilitation center to continue the recovery at home. It counts with dynamic routines through games and interactive ambients that fulfill all knee recovery phase. It provides detail information of every exercise and it correct way of implementation. It offers immediate feedback to ensure the correct way of the exercise.

Move on allows to perform all kind of routines in order to obtain excellent results in knee recovery and thus to increment the treatment adherence of patients.

  • Date: April 2017
  • Client: Universidad Icesi
  • Category: Interactive Installation
  • Software: Java, Processing, Arduino,Firebase
